## Explicit non-goals

- no mandatory dark mode;
- no generated icon sheets; interface icons remain Phosphor;
- no mandatory spectacle or generated imagery;
- no universal serif, palette, punctuation, or centered-layout bans;
- no provider-specific Higgsfield calls in deterministic `ui`;
- no automatic publication or paid generation without appropriate intent/permission;
- no claim that UUIDs replace authorization;
- no copying third-party snippets without dependency, license, security, and compatibility review.

## Exit criteria

- P0 schemas and tests pass all four repository gates.
- D01-D03 each have raw, enhanced, qualified, and art-directed outputs.
- Evaluation is blind and uses durable evaluator references.
- A mechanism is promoted only under the existing learning-record promotion rule.
- Research links point to the pinned inventory; source refreshes are checksum-driven.
