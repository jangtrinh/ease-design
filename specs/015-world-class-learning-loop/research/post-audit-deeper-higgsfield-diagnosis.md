# Post-Audit Deeper Higgsfield Diagnosis

## Why the current work is still below the target

The problem is no longer mainly prompt quality. The preserved pages stop after a written direction
and one implementation. Higgsfield's strongest mechanism is an evidence-producing visual pipeline:
visual section boards, coherent asset generation, section-by-section board fidelity, a focused
motion pass, and a mechanical gate. We adopted the vocabulary earlier but did not execute the
visual artifacts in D01–D03.

## Missing mechanisms, in causal order

1. **No visual grammar before code**
   - Prose direction leaves composition underdetermined.
   - Correction: rendered board per major section, inspected and rerolled before implementation.
2. **No coherent asset-kit selection**
   - D01/D02 contain imagery but no generated/rejected set; D03 avoids media entirely.
   - Correction: production/rejected asset ledger with a coherent palette/grade family.
3. **No board-to-code anti-drift evidence**
   - Qualification proves floor checks, not retained type, spacing, palette, components, or motifs.
   - Correction: fidelity map resolving to both board and render.
4. **No focused motion narrative**
   - Static or hover-only pages miss ambient hero, scroll, loading, and default-state craft.
   - Correction: concept-linked motion plus mobile, initial-frame, screenshot, reduced-motion, and
     JavaScript-failure evidence.
5. **No interactive product states**
   - D02 resembles three screenshots instead of a usable product loop.
   - Correction: real semantics and default/open/selected/disabled/loading/empty/error states with
     keyboard and pointer evidence.
6. **No controlled learning comparison**
   - Without four frozen variants and blind labels, output cannot reveal the causal mechanism.

## Revised execution strategy

Do not repair all three historical pages in place. Start fresh with D01 as a full pipeline probe:

1. freeze prompt, reference, runtime, framework, time, assets, viewports, and rubric;
2. generate raw, enhanced, and first-qualified controls;
3. write Design Read and dials;
4. declare D01 major sections and generate one visual board per section;
5. inspect and reroll generic boards;
6. generate a coherent architecture asset kit and retain rejects;
7. implement the art-directed variant section by section;
8. add concept-linked motion and every floor fallback;
9. capture `390`, `768`, and `1440` renders plus interaction/motion evidence;
10. randomize labels and obtain blind evaluation;
11. only if the workflow is operational, repeat unchanged for D02 and D03.

## Further Higgsfield hypotheses

- board quality is a first-class gate, not optional inspiration;
- section composition varies while retaining one visual grammar;
- one second-read moment rewards attention without obscuring the primary task;
- generated asset candidates are evaluated as a kit, not accepted individually;
- motion comes from the narrative spine, not generic reveal effects;
- initial and reduced-motion frames are designed compositions, not degraded fallbacks.

These remain experiments until controlled D01–D03 evidence supports them.
